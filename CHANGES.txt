zeit.retresco changes
=====================


1.4.2 (unreleased)
------------------

- ZON-3199: Add specific ``Whitelist`` and ``Tag`` to allow type-ahead with
  Retresco.

- ZON-3236: Implement ``ITopicpages`` via the TMS-API.

------------------

- Rename ``acquisition`` to ``access``.
